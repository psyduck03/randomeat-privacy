Privacy Policy — Random Eat

Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Random Eat app ("the app") handles information. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Data We Collect

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking.

Data Stored on Your Device

Recipes and favorites you create are stored only on your device using local storage. This data is never uploaded to any server. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Photos

If you add a photo to your own recipe, the app requests permission to access your device's images. The selected photo is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded or shared.

Internet Access

The app loads meal data and images from public content delivery networks (jsDelivr and Pexels). These requests do not include any personal information about you.

Third-Party Content

Food images are provided by Pexels. Meal data is served via jsDelivr.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children. It is suitable for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page.
